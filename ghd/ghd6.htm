How To Install GHD Sports on Roku Stick

Roku is not an android device and you cannot install the apk file by any means. But don’t need to dishearten yourself because there is a beautiful workaround that we have shared below.

In order to install ghd sports on roku media stick, you need an android phone and allcast app. Just check out the step by step instructions that we have written for you.

1. Download and install ghd sports apk latest version on your android phone. If you have not installed the app yet, you can go up and check out the instructions.

2. Connect the roku streaming stick to your tv.

3. Turn on mobile hotspot on your phone and connect your tv to your mobile wifi connection.

4. Now open roku app store on your tv and install allcast receiver app on your roku stick.

5. Open the ghd app on your smartphone and choose the link that you want to play on your tv. Tap the link and select the option “open with allcast“. You must install allcast application on your phone as well to be able to see that option.

6. Now the allcast app on your phone searches for the device with active allcast receiver installed within the wireless network it is connected in.

7. Once your phone finds your roku stick, you will be able to choose the roku device from you phone to start watching all your favorite live sports matches and movies.
